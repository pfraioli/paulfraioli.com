Profile

Paul Fraioli is a Washington, DC-based expert on the geopolitics of technology, global security and defense, geo-economics, nuclear weapons issues and US foreign policy. He is Senior Fellow for Technology, Geopolitics and Strategy and Editor, Strategic Comments, at the IISS, the world-leading think tank headquartered in London.

His work focuses on how technological innovation is reshaping global power, changing international economic and security relationships, and creating major strategic choices for governments, companies and defence institutions.
